    About Us

    Cafe de Peshawar is a charming and cozy café located in the heart of Peshawar, Pakistan. Known for its warm and inviting ambiance, it offers a delightful fusion of traditional and modern elements. The café serves a variety of flavorful and aromatic local and international cuisine, including aromatic teas, flavorful kebabs, and delectable desserts. It's a popular gathering spot for both locals and tourists, providing a taste of Peshawar's rich culture and culinary traditions in a relaxed and welcoming setting. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to enjoy a cup of tea or a hearty meal with friends, Cafe de Peshawar offers a memorable dining experience in this historic city.
